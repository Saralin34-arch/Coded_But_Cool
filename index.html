About this layout

This page is a neutral wireframe built with plain HTML, CSS, and a small amount of JavaScript. It takes cues from Cargo’s demo layouts: overlapping grey rectangles, plus signs indicating editable areas, and a minimal index on the right side.

Replace the placeholder labels with your own plugin content: images, descriptions, or embedded demos. You can also adjust the positions and sizes of the cards to match your own composition.

Layout principles

  • Large, simple blocks instead of dense UI elements.
  • Overlapping cards to suggest depth and hierarchy.
  • Ample white space around the “canvas” area.
  • Small, light typography in the chrome (header + index).

How to adapt

  • Swap grey cards for images or interactive previews.
  • Map each card to a specific plugin function.
  • Use the side panel for quick access to sections or presets.
  • Extend the layout with more cards or a grid if needed.

Implementation notes

The JavaScript on this page is intentionally minimal: a small tooltip helper for explaining cards on hover, and a scroll-reveal effect that fades cards in as they enter the viewport. All layout is handled with straightforward CSS, so you can easily move this into your own repo or integrate it into a more complex UI.
